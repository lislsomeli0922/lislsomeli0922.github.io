Best to Try On Outfits That You Can't Wear Because of COVID

If the energy is high in the family then we will do a mini fashion show. My sister has a lot of fun styling us and also being the main attraction. I love getting to wear outfits that I spent a lot of money on during mental breakdowns throughout the pandemic and haven't gotten the chance to wear out.

Mia Says..! I like dresses the most, and ones that are really long are my favorite :)

How to Make a Runway at Home:

  1. Clear out a space that is long enough
  2. Put a decorative blanket/s to create the runway itself
  3. Use small stuffed animals as the audience around the blankets
  4. Add fairy lights to the outline of the runway
  5. Put a speaker behind the runway
  6. Turn the music up and the fairy lights on
  7. Models strut the runway!

This is great to take her mind of things that trigger her and also do something other than being on the phone. We all have so much fun and just try to create a theme to make it even more fun.
