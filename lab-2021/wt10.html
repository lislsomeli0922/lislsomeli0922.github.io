Filming Tik Toks

Difficult But Entertaining

It should be no surprise that my sister loves filming Tik Toks. I mean who doesn't? It has definitely become more popular and common during the pandemic and it is really entertaining. Its really fun to do the challenges and transitions to for our amazing makeup skills.

How to Film a Tik Tok:

  1. Open up the recording tab
  2. Choose a specific sound for your video
  3. Choose between 15 or 60 seconds for the duration
  4. Add a filter if needed
  5. Use the timer for specific times you want the filming to stop
  6. Use a transititon you learned or practiced
  7. Finish shooting the video
  8. Review and add captions
  9. Finish and add a caption to post
  10. Post!
