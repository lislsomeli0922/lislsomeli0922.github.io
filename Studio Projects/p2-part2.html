Sketches on InDesign

For this idea, I wanted to imitate a flower information book. Each latin flower name represents a certain part of the text
and when hovered over each name the appropriate part of the text pops up. Flowers recieve their name for a purpose,
whether it be the mood they give off or what they symbolize.




This design is meant to represent the overwheming feeling the text stresses of growing
and how nothing will ever be as beautiful as the original "garden".




The flower name again represents a certain part of the text and the text would fade into
full view depending on the structure of the text and the tempo it should be read.
