Project 1:Cross-Platform Storytelling

Cinderella based on the story by Brothers Grimm

Twitter

A Twitter thread with gifs allows for a short retelling
of a specific moment with light hearted visual aid.

Instagram

An Instagram profile highlights significant moments in the story and
hashtags showcase popularity and a new "language" created from
social media.

Uber

Uber shows the importance of transportation in the story and
exclusively tells the story through those movements.
